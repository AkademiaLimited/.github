## 1. Purpose

We expect all participants to treat each other with respect and professionalism.

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for all, regardless of gender, sexual orientation, ability, ethnicity, socioeconomic status, and religion (or lack thereof).

This Code of Conduct outlines our expectations for all members of our company and our collaborators, as well as the consequences for unacceptable behavior.

Together, we strive to create a safe and positive experience for everyone.

## 2. Expected Behavior

The following behaviors are expected of all members and collaborators:

* Exercise consideration and respect in your speech and actions.
* Attempt collaboration before conflict.
* Refrain from demeaning, discriminatory, or harassing behavior and speech.
* Be mindful of your surroundings and of your fellow participants. Alert company leadership if you notice a dangerous situation, someone in distress, or violations of this Code of Conduct, even if they seem inconsequential.

## 3. Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are considered violations of this Code of Conduct:

* Harassment and discrimination: We do not tolerate any form of harassment or discrimination. This includes, but is not limited to, offensive comments, jokes, or actions related to gender, sexual orientation, ability, ethnicity, socioeconomic status, religion, or any other personal characteristics.
* Inappropriate conduct: This includes unwelcome sexual attention, intimidation, stalking, and the display or distribution of inappropriate or offensive content in any form.
* Sustained disruption: Habitual disruption of meetings, discussions, or work-related activities that hinders the collaboration of others.
* Advocating for or encouraging any of the above behavior.

Inappropriate behavior will not be tolerated, whether it occurs within the workplace, during business trips, at company-related social events, or through online communication channels.

## 4. Reporting Guidelines

If you are subject to or witness unacceptable behavior, or have any other concerns, please notify @genszk as soon as possible.

All reports will be handled with confidentiality and discretion to protect the privacy of those involved. We are committed to addressing every report seriously and taking appropriate action to ensure a safe work environment.

## 5. Enforcement and Consequences

Anyone asked to stop unacceptable behavior is expected to comply immediately.

If a participant engages in behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, the company may take any action they deem appropriate, including but not limited to:

* A formal warning
* Removal from a specific project or event
* Disciplinary action in accordance with company work rules (Employment Regulations)
